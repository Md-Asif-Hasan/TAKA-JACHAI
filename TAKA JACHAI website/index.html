Important Disclaimer

Taka Jachai is a screening tool, not a legal authority. The app's results are not legally conclusive and should never be your sole basis for action.

If you suspect a note is counterfeit:

🏦

Bangladesh Bank

Visit your nearest Bangladesh Bank branch or authorized commercial bank

👮

Bangladesh Police

Emergency Helpline: 999

Possessing or circulating counterfeit currency is a criminal offense under Bangladesh law. Always report suspected counterfeits to the police.
