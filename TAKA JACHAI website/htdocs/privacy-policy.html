Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Taka Jachai is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

  • • Email address and display name (used for account creation and identification)
  • • Scan history — denomination, prediction result, confidence score, and date
  • • Profile information you choose to provide: bio, avatar selection
  • • Leaderboard statistics: total scans, genuine scans, counterfeits caught

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • • Captured banknote images are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server
  • • We do not collect location data, device identifiers, or browsing history
  • • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising

How We Use Your Data

  • • To authenticate your account via Firebase Authentication
  • • To store and sync your scan history and profile via Firebase Firestore
  • • To display your ranking on the public leaderboard (only aggregated stats, no images)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can read or write your own profile and scan history. Leaderboard entries are publicly readable but contain only your display name, avatar, and scan statistics.

Your Rights

  • • You may delete your account at any time from Settings → Danger Zone, which permanently removes all your data from our servers
  • • You may control what is visible on your public profile via Settings → Privacy
  • • You may request a copy of your data by contacting us at asifhasan10122000@gmail.com

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase's privacy policy applies to data processed through their services: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
